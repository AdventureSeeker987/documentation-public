---
title: "Market Data"
---

`@orderly.network/hooks` provides the following hooks related to market data:

- [useMarketsStream](/sdks/hooks/advanced/use-markets-stream) - Retreive all listed symbols and its market data
- [useMarkPrice](/sdks/hooks/advanced/use-mark-price) - Get the mark price of a single symbol
- [useMarkPricesStream](/sdks/hooks/advanced/use-mark-prices-stream) - Get the mark price of all symbols
- [useMarketTradeStream](/sdks/hooks/advanced/use-market-trade-stream) - Get the market trades history of a symbol
