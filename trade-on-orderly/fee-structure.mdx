---
title: "Fee Structure"
---

Orderly implemented a tiered fee structure based on a 30-day volume (separately for Spot and Futures). The fee structure can also be retrieved with our REST API.

<Note>
Fees are subject to change at any time.
</Note>

## Perpetual Futures Trading Fees

### EVM & NEAR

| Tier | 30-day Futures volume | Maker  | Taker  |
| ---- | --------------------- | ------ | ------ |
| 1    | \$0 - \$500K            | 0.030% | 0.060% |
| 2    | \$500K - \$2.5M          | 0.024% | 0.054% |
| 3    | \$2.5M-\$10M             | 0.018% | 0.048% |
| 4    | \$10M-\$100M            | 0.012% | 0.042% |
| 5    | \$100M-\$250M           | 0.006% | 0.040% |
| 6    | Above \$250M           | 0.000% | 0.040% | 
<Note>
Future trading fees are collected in USDC.
</Note>



## Spot Trading Fees

<Note>
Brokers also have the ability to set custom fees. Please [reach out](mailto:support@orderly.network) to us if you are looking to set custom fees for your platform.
</Note>

### NEAR

| Tier | 30-day Spot volume | Maker  | Taker  |
| ---- | ------------------ | ------ | ------ |
| 1    | \$0-\$500K           | 0.100% | 0.100% |
| 2    | \$500K-\$3M          | 0.080% | 0.090% |
| 3    | \$3M-\$10M           | 0.060% | 0.080% |
| 4    | \$10M-\$50M          | 0.040% | 0.070% |
| 5    | \$50M-\$100M         | 0.020% | 0.060% |
| 6    | Above \$100M        | 0.000% | 0.050% |

<Note>
Spot trading fees are deducted from the converted token (e.g. converting USDC to ETH will incur fees in ETH)
</Note>
